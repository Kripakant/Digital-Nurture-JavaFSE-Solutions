Community Bulletin

The city council is pleased to announce a packed calendar of events this season. Residents of all ages are encouraged to take part in the many free and low-cost activities planned throughout the summer months.

New this year is an expanded farmers market featuring more than forty local vendors, a dedicated children's craft corner, and live acoustic performances every Saturday morning in the town square.

Volunteers remain the heart of our community programme. If you would like to help organise an event, lead a workshop, or simply lend a hand on the day, please reach out to the events office.

For accessibility requests, parking information, and weather updates, check the portal regularly. We look forward to seeing you at an event soon!
